Help Centre

General

Welcome to Ushelf Help Centre, feel free to contact us via email and WhatsApp if you have any doubts or questions.

Q: What is Ushelf?

A: Ushelf is the C2C and B2C platform for storage renting. You can rent our your idle storage space or rent from other.

Q: What type of storage do you have?

A: We have normal storage, frozen storage and wine storage.

Q: What size of storage do you have?

A: Every host has different unit sizes, but to brings convenience to you for searching, we had categorised the size into small (16sqft - 25sqft), medium (26sqft - 50sqft), large (51sqft - 75sqft), extra large(above 76sqft).

Q: Is moving and packing services provided?

A: While we do not actually provide moving and packaging services, we may be able to refer you to some movers. Moreover, some host are providing such services as special features that you may prefer to have it.

Booking & Payment

Q: Can I make a booking?

A: Yes! But first, please sign up or log in to your Ushelf account to proceed the booking.

Q: How do I make a booking?

A: Search the favourite storage on Ushelf platform, and when you are ready, check the storage details, select the start time and end time on the calendar. Finally, click the "Book" button to place the booking. The host will respond to your booking in 24 hours or it will be expired.

Q: I had place the booking but it say it is wait for confirm? What does that means?

A: Every booking require host's confirmation for accept or reject, your understanding and patience is greatly appreciated.

Q: What should I do if my booking is accepted?

A: After the booking is accepted, please proceed to your "MyShelf" and make the payment under the "Wait for payment" category. If the payment is successful, the whole order is completed.

Q: What should I do if my booking is rejected?

A: The host may reject your booking in some reasons, hence, we will suggest you to search other storage space on Ushelf.

Q: What are the modes of payment?

A: Ushelf accept online payment via VISA and MasterCard.

Storing and Picking

Q: When can I access my storage space?

A: Please contact your host for further information in accessing. You can message your host in the "In Rental" in "MyShelf"

Q: What are the prohibited for storage?

A: Live animals or plants, perishables items, explosive items, toxic items, fuel, and any items prohibited or controlled by law. For more information, please Click here. Moreover, there are other different items that may be prohibited by the host, such as furniture, dolls and more.

Q: The host always reject to let me access to storage space, what should I do?

A: Please contact Ushelf customer service for further assiatance.
