Storing Rule

Most household items, business items and commercial items can be stored in the storage space. Examples of things are furniture, documents, books, commercial stock, wine, sports equipment etc. But please take note that different host has different storage rule, please read the section "Things to keep in mind" in the storage detail you make your choice.

Few items are prohibited in all storage space, which includes:

  • Live animals/plants
  • Perishables items (such as foodstuffs)
  • Explosive items
  • Flammable gases/liquids
  • Fuel/diesel
  • Toxic/corrosive/oxidizing materials
  • Any items prohibited or controlled by law (such as weapons, drugs)
