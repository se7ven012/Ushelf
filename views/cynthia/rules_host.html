Rules for Host

General

Q: Can I become a host?

A: If you have idle storage space is 16sqft or above, and willing to rent out, Yes!

Q: How to become a host?

A: Switch to Renting and click the "Getting started" to upload your first listings. After complete the listings, you will become a Ushelf host.

Q: Anything I need to do as a host?

A: Keep your storage ready and always welcome your renter. Click the "Rules" on the left bar to read more.

Rules

As a Ushelf host, few rules you need to be take note of

  • Be responsive. Respond the booking in 24 hours, either accept or reject. If you don't make any response, you will get a warning from Ushelf and get blacklisted if this happened three times.
  • Be honest to your renter and us, your account will get banned if any fraud happens.
  • Be resposibility, keep the storage items in safe. You will get fined or sue for item lost or damaged.
  • Avoid rejection, we take rejection seriously and will get warning if you reject too many bookings.

Your listings

Q: I can't provide any special features listed in the storage form, can I still upload it?

A: Yes, the special features is additional features that the host can provided. It doesn't affect your upload process.

Q: Where can I manage my listings?

A: You will see the "MyListings" after switching to host. You can manage all your listings there.

Q: Can I remove the listings?

A: Yes, you can remove the listings in the "MyListings". Click for more details and there is a button "remove". But be caution that the remove action can not be undo, please think twice before you remove it.

Q: I just remove some listings, how can I recover it back?

A: Basicallly, the removed listings cannot be recover, but please contact Ushelf customer service for further assistance.

Your order

Q: Can I accept the booking?

A: Yes, you can accept the booking.

Q: Can I reject the booking?

A: Yes, you can reject the booking. However, if you reject too many bookings, the Ushelf will give you the warnings.

Q: What happen if I do not respond the booking?

A: The booking will automatically reject after 24 hours and you will get a warning from Ushelf. If this happened three times, you will get blacklisted.

Q: What can I do when the booking is wait for payment?

A: Nothing you can do much, but you can prepare your storage space be ready for the prospective renter.
